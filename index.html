Crossroads Rhode Island's 2016 Outcomes


What is Crossroads Rhode Island?

Crossroads Rhode Island is the state's largest provider of services to the homeless.

The mission of Crossroads Rhode Island is to help homeless and at-risk familes and individuals secure stable homes. Those we serve achieve this by engaging in our range of services including housing, emergency shelter, basic needs, and education and employment services.

What did we do in 2016?

Last year was an important one at Crossroads, as we expanded several of our programs and services:

  • The Domestic Violence Program of Crossroads was established following a three-year affiliation with the Women's Center of Rhode Island.

  • We completed the renovation of Whitmarsh House, which provides supportive housing for 11 chronically homeless or youth individuals.

  • We began operating Harrington Hall, the largest shelter for men in the state.

  • We expanded our Street Outreach Program aimed at providing housing and supportive services to the unsheltered homeless in our community.

Through support from our donors, we help our clients overcome barriers and ultimately help them end their homeless circumstances with stable housing.

How to Help

The easiest and most effective way that you can help us work toward our mission is to donate via our website.

We also accept in-kind donations of toiletries, furniture, and new clothing when we have an immediate need for them. Due to a lack of storage space, we are unfortunately not able to accept in-kind donations on an ongoing basis.
